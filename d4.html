Project information

  • Title: Sav-id (Save Small Business from Covid-19)
  • Category: Application (Android)
  • Project date: 2020
  • Download URL: www.sav-id.com

Description

Sav-id (Save Small Business from Covid-19) is a digital economy application developed as a forum for the younger generation to help save (save) home businesses and UMKM during a pandemic by selling and promoting the product.

The goal is to help small businesses survive the pandemic and develop in a sustainable manner and provide opportunities for young people to play a role in the digital economy. I developed this application in collaboration with Udayana University Health Faculty students
